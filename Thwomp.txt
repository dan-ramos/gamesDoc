====== Thwomp ======
Created Thursday 10 August 2023

Doodle jump but backwards

Points based? Bonus points for going out of your  way, each fall grants certain number of points

Whomp starts at the top of [[Thwomp:Level|the level which loops downwards]] as he climbs down it. Level generates until he hits a trampoline which takes him to [[Thwomp:Level:Building Types|a new location theme]]. Game ends when [[Thwomp:Level:Motivation|whomp gets whomped]].
